Medical Development

New Generic Drug for Diabetes Management

Our R&D department has developed a new generic formulation of Metformin with extended release properties, offering better blood sugar control for Type 2 diabetes patients. Clinical trials show improved patient compliance due to once-daily dosing.

This development aligns with our mission to make diabetes management more accessible and affordable for the growing diabetic population in Bangladesh.

Event

Free Health Camp Organized in Rural Areas

KHAN Pharmaceuticals organized a free health camp in Chuadanga district, providing free consultations and medications to over 500 patients. The camp included blood pressure screening, diabetes testing, and general health check-ups.

This is part of our ongoing commitment to community health and wellness. We plan to organize similar camps across Bangladesh throughout 2026.

December 15, 2025
Medical Periodical

Understanding Antibiotic Resistance

Antibiotic resistance is becoming a major global health concern. Our medical team has published a comprehensive guide on the proper use of antibiotics and the dangers of self-medication.

Key recommendations include: completing the full course of antibiotics, never sharing prescribed medications, and consulting healthcare professionals before taking antibiotics.
