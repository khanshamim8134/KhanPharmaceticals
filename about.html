About KHAN Pharmaceuticals PLC.

KHAN Pharmaceuticals PLC. incorporated as a limited company in 2025, received its regulatory approval in October 2025 to operate as a pharmaceutical company to manufacture and market medicine in Bangladesh. The company has been built on the pillars of integrity, knowledge, respect and trust.

We operate at high ethical standards and are committed towards building the organization on the foundation of our vision, mission and philosophy. At KHAN Pharmaceuticals PLC, we aspire to aid our customers in leading a healthy life by formulating, developing, commercializing quality medicine and delivering affordable and accessible medicines that satisfy their urgent medical needs.

We continuously strive for the success of our company through research, innovation, training & development, manufacture and marketing of safe and effective medicines. We consider our employees as most valuable assets that can make our dreams come true. By listening to our employees and acting on what we hear, we identify and build on the strengths and talents of our people and make KHAN Pharmaceuticals a better place to work at.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, affordable pharmaceutical products and healthcare solutions that improve the quality of life for people in Bangladesh and beyond. We are committed to excellence in research, manufacturing, and customer service.

Our Vision

We aspire to:

  • Develop a broad portfolio of products and become a significant national player in the generics arena
  • Emerge as a leading national player in high quality generic formulations
  • Grow by expanding territory throughout the country and internationally
  • Be recognized as a trusted healthcare partner by healthcare professionals and patients
  • Create sustainable value for all our stakeholders through innovation and operational excellence

Milestones of Excellence

  • 2025 - Foundation: KHAN Pharmaceuticals PLC. established with state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Chuadanga, Bangladesh
  • October 2025 - Regulatory Approval: Received approval from Bangladesh Drug Administration to manufacture and market pharmaceutical products
  • March 2025 - Commercial Production: Started commercial production with 15 essential medicines
  • 2025 - Quality Certification: Achieved WHO-GMP certification for manufacturing facility
  • 2026 - Product Portfolio: Expanded product range to over 50 formulations across multiple therapeutic categories
  • 2026 - Market Expansion: Established distribution network covering all 64 districts of Bangladesh
